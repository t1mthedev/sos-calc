INFORMATION[]

Behemoth MK III is a Companion Behemoth. Upon acquisition, Companion Behemoths grant global stat bonuses. Their effects on Marches are purely cosmetic and apply to all Marches. To upgrade Behemoth MK III you will need Power Serum, Neuronal Medium, and Aero Cell. These items can be gotten from Companion Intel. Behemoth III offers war stats.
